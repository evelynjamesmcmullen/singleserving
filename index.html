SHOP

  • Weekday

    Weekday is a Swedish brand, but it has gained prominence in many of its neighboring Scandinavian countries, including Denmark. They focus on cool minimalist streetwear, but also come out with some fun statement pieces as well. The best part about it is that you can't purchase anything from them from the US, so nobody will jack your style :-)

  • Zara Home

    We all know Zara, but what is Zara Home? The answer is a magical place that does not yet exist in the United States that includes any piece of fun home decor that you could ever conjure up in your mind. I'm talking monocrhomatic cutlery, skull candles, giant fur pillows, the works.

  • Freetown Christiania

    Whether you smoke weed or not, Christiania is a very cool place to go. It's an independent "anarchist district", where it's residents don't live by the rules of the EU or the Danish government. This has led it to be a popular spot to buy and sell Marijuana and Hash on the long street that passes through, otherwise known as 'pusher street'. There are small art galleries around the area, leashless dogs, and very interesting people with great stories. Unfortunately, I don't have any pictures of Christiania as it is against their rules
